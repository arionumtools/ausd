FAQ

1.What is the difference between AUSD and other USD stablecoin?

We issue a corresponding amount of AUSD based on other stablecoin,just like originally issued US dollars based on gold reserve.

1 AUSD is always equal to 1 USD Stablecoin.

Holding AUSD can avoid the risk of a single stablecoin, we take this risk.

2.Why should I trust AUSD?

Reserve stablecoin balance is public,everyone can verify in real time.

We will open source the exchange process program on github.

3.What is Worn Wallet, is my money still safe?

The money is safe,this is for privacy consideration.

Wallet older than 3 months and used more than 100 times will be marked as worn.

Although the address is anonymous, but all transactions are an open book in internet.

You can continue to use it, but just like a real wallet, you need to replace it.

4.With multiple addresses, can I use the HD wallet?

We do not use HD wallets,this only provides a false sense of privacy and security.

The person who gets your seed can take all your money and can view the transactions of all addresses generated by that seed.

No one will guarantee that the seed will not be leaked in the future for any reason.

5. I need to back up every wallet, right?

This is something you have to do, is your money, you need to do this.

Set up different backup methods for different wallets. If one wallet leaks, the money in the other wallets is safe.

You can delete a wallet after you save the private key.

It’s like paper money, but it’s smaller and lighter,only you know it when you hide it.

Delete worn wallet so that no one can know you own that wallet. over time, all transactions in that wallet are irrelevant to you.

6. Is Fast payment safe? Why doesn't Bitcoin treat Mempool transactions as paid?

The payment is safe, because the transaction fee is fixed, so the payer cannot pay a higher fee to invalidate the unconfirmed transaction.

For large transaction, please wait for more confirmations to prevent 51% attack.

7. Why do I need to wait a minute before I can make a payment?

This is because the wallet does not have enough AUSDC, and the exchange AUSDC require 1 block confirmation.

We have the corresponding AUSD exchange address, keep some AUSDC for payment, can avoid this situation.
